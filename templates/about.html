About Hunter Gatherer


What am I looking at?

Product Hunt is a community-driven platform. Hunter Gatherer is a tool to explore key influencers in your community who can help your product succeed.

How is this information useful to me?

You can maximize the number of votes for your product if it is posted by someone with high community reputation. The graphs help you identify the biggest clusters of influencers in your network.

Who are you monsters?

We are the team behind Sand Hill Exchange, a fantasy stock exchange for startups.

How are these graphs generated?

We compile a list of posts and votes for each individual. The networks are determined by whom you have voted for, and who has voted for your posts.

Tighter connections indicate that two nodes have voted for each others' posts a lot. In the example below, hunterwalk and rrhoover have a very tight connection, but eriktorenberg is farther away from hunterwalk. If eriktorenberg wants to get hunterwalk's attention, he should have rrhoover post his product :)

If we explore rrhoover's network below, we see that he has a giant cluster of highly-connected followers, and then a bunch of fanboys floating at the outskirts of his network (at the top of the graph).
